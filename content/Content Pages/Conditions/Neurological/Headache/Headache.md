# Types of Headaches

## Primary Headaches
Occur Spontaneously and do not signify underlying pathophysiology
- [[Migraine]] - Regular, severe throbbing headache with or without aura
- [[Tension-type headaches]] - Most common headache, ache both sides of front of head, consider carbon monoxide poisoning
- [[Cluster Headaches]] - 'clusters' of extremely painful headaches on one side

## Secondary Headaches
Caused by underlying pathophysiology
- [[subarachnoid haemorrhage]] - Headache like being hit, worst ever, bleed on the brain
- [[Medication-Overuse Headaches]] - analgesic resistant headache
