# Double Crewed Ambulance - DCA

- Designed to stabilise, assess and transport patients, the most common form of ambulance
- Can be crewed by 2 EACs (or equivalent), but this often restricts the calls they can be sent to
- Typically crewed by EAC (or equivalent role) and Health Care Professional (most commonly a paramedic / senior paramedic)
