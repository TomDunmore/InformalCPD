# Rapid Response Vehicle - RRV

- Typically a car with similar equipment to ambulances, but not designed to transport patients (in most circumstances)
- Almost entirely crewed by a single Health Care Professional (most commonly paramedics) and mainly used by NHS trust services
- Typically used to provide rapid delivery of advanced care to critically unwell patients, or responding to patients with urgent, but not emergency, care needs that would not require hospital transfer
