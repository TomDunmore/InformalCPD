# Pre-alerts

Calling ahead to a hospital so they can prepare for the arrival of an unwell patient
- Different criteria and guidelines depending on local area
- Directly or indirectly passes:
	- Patient Age and sex
	- Brief History
	- Brief Injuries / Illnesses
	- Interventions
	- Full set of Observations
	- Estimate time of arrival to hospital
