# Page 31-32 User Manual of [[Power-Pro XT#^5ef3ac|Power-Pro XT]]
- Remove existing restraints, sit stretcher up
- Place Pedi-mate with straps out on backrest of stretcher
- wrap rear straps around and through brackets on backrest
- Wrap 2 lower straps around stretcher frame on each side, fasten to itself
- Tighten straps to secure to stretcher and use as per a child car seat
