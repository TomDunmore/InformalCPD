# What is it?
The 'main bag' taken to almost every patient. The internal layout often segmented into pouches mirroring A-E approach
Items within are either:
- Commonly Needed
	- Observation kit
	- Simple dressings
- necessary to respond to time-critical presentation
	- Tourniquets and packing
	- Oxygen and bag valve masks
	- Airway management
	- Time critical medications (dependent on service policy)
