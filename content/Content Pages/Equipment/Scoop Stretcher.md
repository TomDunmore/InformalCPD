# What is it?
- Extendable, rigid carried stretcher that can split vertically into 2 pieces
- Used to retrieve patients from the floor, or for immobilisation
- For longer term immobilisation, consider using a [[Vacuum Mattress]]
# Evidence
- Evidence (funded by manufacturer) supports use of scoop compared to backboards by reducing movement and being more comfortable [@krell2006comparisonfernoscoop]
