# Usage of features (Refer to user  manual)
## Side rails
- To put up, move up and sides will lock into position
- To lower, squeeze red handle on side, put slight pressure up and then lower fully
## Shortening Stretcher (Page 56)
- To shorten, squeeze red handles on frame under backrest and push into stretcher
- To lengthen, squeeze the same red handles and pull
## Raising legs
- To Angle legs, pull up red loops by knees and pull up
- to lift legs, pull red tab on foot end of stretcher and lift leg section
- To lower both, pull same red tab on foot end and lower any raised areas of the legs
## Raising backrest (page 36)
- To raise, squeeze red handle behind backrest for pneumatic assist in lifting the backrest to the desired height. 
- To lower, squeeze red handle behind backrest and push down on the backrest frame until the backrest has reached the desired height.
