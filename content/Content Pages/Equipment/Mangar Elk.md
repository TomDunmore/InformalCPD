# Lifting Cushions
- Manual Handling aid to support patient from floor (often after a fall) with minimal moving and handling
- Inserted under a patient using rolling and then inflated with air compressor
- Noted to be used in only 6% of falls in care homes, despite access to lifting cushions [@houghton2024implementationimpactlifting]
- Instructions below or in [@pilbery2019ambulancecarepractice]
