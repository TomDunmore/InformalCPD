# What is it?
- Immobilisation and extrication device from sitting position
- Main use case is extrication from vehicles
# Evidence
- Concerns have been raised by @brown2015shouldkendrickextrication as to the benefit of this due to:
	- Unfamiliarity with rare use
	- Time taken to apply
	- Movement during application
	- Potential for restriction on breathing
