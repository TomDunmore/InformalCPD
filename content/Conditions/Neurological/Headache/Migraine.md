#Condition 
As seen in @jrcalc2020headachejrcalcguideline

- Affects about 15% of UK Adults
- 3x more common in women
- Wide variety of presentations, but often will be known to the person

# Migraine with Aura
- About 33% of Migraine patients
- Warning sign before migraine begins, can include:
	- light sensitivity
	- neck, shoulder or limb stiffness
	- altered limb sensation or weakness
- Can be a stroke mimic. If in doubt, escalate

# Migraine without Aura
- Headache as normal, but no aura as described above

# Migraine without Headache (Silent Migraine)
- aura as normal, but no headache
- Can be a stroke mimic. If in doubt, escalate
